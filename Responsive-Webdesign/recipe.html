Dhal Bhat

Everyone's favorite Dhal Bhat fully healthy.

Ingredients

  • 50g mog or moosor dhal
  • 200g rice
  • 400g potato
  • 4 pice raw chili
  • 4 pice garlic
  • 1 onion
  • one Spoon Paanch Phoran

Instructions

  1. boil dhal, rice, potato
  2. cut onion and put that side
  3. mix termeric onto dhal and boil a bit then add paanch Phoran 2 chili and 3 pice garlic in one spoon musterted oil, then put that onto dhal
  4. mix salt bit oil 2 remaining chili 1 pice garlic then all set if you need more information email: gopalmahatoinfo@gmail.com
